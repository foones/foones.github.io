Komix


I did some comics in the past, as another complement to my constructed worlds.
As always, I started with a joke, the Chabones Rangers. The title: It's a mix of the Power Rangers (which I would hate) and the Argentinian Slang word "chabón". Chabón means "unskillful", but it's used as a common vocative or just as "guy".
The comics I did (around 1996) were horrible (REALLY horrible). Their scripts are bad jokes, the stories last two pages and the drawings... better not to ask.
But as with the religion, the things started being a joke, continued as a serious job. I did then lots of better comics (I won't say they're good, cause they're almost all copies of better works from other people with another characters and with a lot of modified details). I continued doing comics as a hobby till 1999, when I stopped doing them. I created another characters (not just the "Chabones Rangers"), as Forman, Hiperman, The Mutants and others. Some comics are really a mess, cause they travel to different times, places, "dimensions", "destinies" and "alternative days"... AHHHH!!!
But what I liked most is telling my own religion facts in the comics (in example, I use the primal god of Dwarfism in stories, as a friend of the characters, helping them to do missions).
